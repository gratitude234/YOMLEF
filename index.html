Who We Are

At YOMLEF (Young Millionaire Leaders Forum), we are dedicated to shaping the next generation of leaders through comprehensive training programs and entrepreneurial opportunities. Our goal is to equip young individuals with leadership skills that empower them to build successful, purpose-driven careers. Alongside our leadership training, YOMLEF also engages in product sales such as groundnut, allowing us to provide practical business experience and support for our mission.

Our Vision

To nurture and empower young minds, providing them with the skills, knowledge, and opportunities to become future leaders and innovators.

Our Mission

Through structured training platforms, seminars, and conferences, YOMLEF equips individuals with tools to create purpose-driven startups and lead impactful lives.

Our Core Values

    • Innovation
    • Leadership
    • Personal Development
    • Creativity
    • Community Empowerment
